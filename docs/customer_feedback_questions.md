# Marketing OS Lite — Customer Feedback Questions

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-05-02  
**Use:** Post-demo feedback session (5–10 minutes)  
**Format:** Verbal interview or written form  
**Audience:** Marketing manager, agency owner, or growth lead who attended the demo

---

## How to Use This Document

Read each question aloud after the demo. Take notes in the **Response** field. If conducting async (written form), share this file as a PDF or Google Doc with the response fields highlighted.

Do not lead the witness — let the customer finish their thought before asking follow-up questions. The prompts in *italics* are optional follow-ups, not scripted replies.
